Sweet Restaurant Slaw

Learn the secret to that sweet restaurant-style slaw and make it in the comfort of your own home.

No need to go out to fill that sweet slaw craving anymore.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag coleslaw mix
  • 2 tablespoons diced onion
  • 2/3 cup creamy salad dressing
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon poppy seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Combine coleslaw mix in large bowl.
  2. Whisk salad dressing, sugar, vegetable oil, vinegar, poppy seeds, and salt together in medium bowl until blended. Pour dressing over coleslaw mix and toss to coat.
  3. Chill for at least 2 hours before serving.
  4. That's it! Enjoy!
