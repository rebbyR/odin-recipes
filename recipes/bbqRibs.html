Simple To Make BBQ Ribs

These ribs are simple to make and don't require the use of a grill!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 pounds country-style pork ribs
  • 2 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 cup barbeque sauce

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Place ribs in a large pot and cover with water. Stir in kosher salt, garlic powder, and pepper. Bring to a boil until ribs are tender.
  3. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F while ribs are boiling.
  4. Remove ribs from pot and place them in a baking dish. Pour BBQ sauce over ribs and cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake until pork has reached 160 degrees F.
  6. Enjoy!
